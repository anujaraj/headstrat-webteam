About Us

HeadStart- RMSoEE Student Cell is the student representative body of Rajendra Misra School Of Engineering Entrepreneurship (RMSoEE), IIT Kharagpur and is the only cell of its kind in the campus which is meant for promoting the objectives of RMSoEE which is nurturing the potential start-up ideas that will cater need of both tech and non-tech solution to various social, economic and industrial problems.
HeadStart is the pulpit for young entrepreneurs to effectuate excellence, innovation, and scrupulousness
